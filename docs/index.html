Opening Monero to the world

Haveno-reto is the flagship instance of Haveno, the non-custodial, decentralized exchange platform for crypto and fiat currencies built on Tor and Monero.

It doesn't get more private than that.

What Haveno-reto is

The first fully functional Haveno instance on mainnet

A low arbitration fee network - 0% taker, 0.6% maker

A network with zero general fees - fees go exclusively to arbitrators

Essentially Bisq 1 adapted for Monero

What Haveno-reto is not

Custodial

Centralized

The developers of the Haveno software

Officially endorsed by the Haveno developers - no instance is endorsed

PSA - Network Issues Alert

Jul 19, 2024

We are currently experiencing some issues due to a DDoS attack on the seed nodes. The trade experience itself is not impacted once you are connected to the Haveno-reto network and your orderbook has loaded.

You may encounter the following:

Delayed startup

Launching Haveno might take longer than usual.

Incomplete order book

You may initially see a partial order book or receive a "filter object error." The order book will load in with time, but this can take up to an hour. The filter object error can be ignored for now.

Recommendations:

Please be patient during the startup and synchronization process.

If possible, keep your client open to maintain network connectivity.

For the best experience, consider leaving Haveno running in the background when not in use.

We are in the process of migrating seed nodes to Tor PoW protection. These issues should be resolved in the coming weeks.
